I worked with Professor [Xiangxiang Zeng](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=B20HBMIAAAAJ&hl=en) and [Bosheng Song](https://scholar.google.com.hk/citations?user=n2szdRoAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN).
